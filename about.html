About

Meet the designer

Rafia Begum

Born in City of London, UK, 1990​.

Rafia is the founder of Rafia Design. She has always been captivated with the Art & Design biosphere; having studied it since her early teens she has experienced many aspects within the world of design. Her design style and contagious enthusiasm has seen her gain gratitude, including being awarded a £5,000 Artbeat scholarship by the American Law Firm ‘Allen & Overy.’

She graduated with a Ba(Hons) degree in Interior Design from the University of the Arts London. In that time she has gained the confidence to pursue concepts that challenge her creativity and has expanded her knowledge across the many fields of design. She has also developed numerous practical skills and qualities, all of which she believes can improve an individual's quality of life. Through the use of innovative and intelligent design, Rafia sees the possibility of positive change happening all around us.

She thrives on inspiration from nature and her daily surroundings; creating designs that are not only beautiful but also multi-functional. Her sincere passion and mission is to create inspiring designs that motivates and enhances the lives of others.
