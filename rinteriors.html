What I offer.

Offering (concept) Interior Design service for residential homes in London, with an option of three different packages to choose from:


* FREE 1 Hour CONSULTATION – INTRODUCTION meeting at property.

  • Package 1

    Design Advice

  • Per room *
  • Comprises:

  • 2 meetings including (2 hour) extended consultation & 1 hour presentation of one design solution via Mood Board including simple layout plan of furniture with sourcing details for two items of choice*. Plus tips on where to best source products/soft furnishing and home accessories that match your personal budget.

  • Package 2

    Styling/Design Scheme

  • Per room *
  • Comprises:

  • 4 meetings, (excluding consultation*) 1 hour each, provided with 2 Design schemes at first initial meeting, visual Mood Boards & Specification of all chosen furniture, including sourcing 4 top items of choice.*

  • Package 3

    Partial Project Management

  • Per room *
  • Comprises:

  • Meetings depending on progress. Max 5 in total. Working with your builders on implementing the chosen 'R Interiors' design scheme. Helping with the final touches at the end of all building work; adding soft furnishing and accessories to finalise the design and complete the service.

*for further details and prices please feel free to contact me.

I offer interior design services for residential properties. My focus is on space that lifts the spirits as well as stimulates and indulges the senses. I want the interiors to evoke a mood and create a space where the five senses are enticed. I explore your home and translate your ideas into a tangible concept that highlight on elements such as colours, materials, texture, mass, scale, lighting and balance; creating unity and a narrative. All these elements will follow their own rules and restrictions. I believe strongly that a good designer is a designer who knows when to break the rules. Thus, I know the difference between “Less is more” - Mies van der Rohe and “Less is a bore” - Robert Venturi. I craft all my designs according to the style preferences and personality of my clients so each design is one-of-a-kind.


My mission is very transparent; I want to deliver your design vision with utmost integrity and commitment according to your needs and requirements. I like to create relationships spatially and interpersonally enhancing the performance of the built environment and its inhabitants; invigorating faith and experience. Comfort and luxury combined with simplicity and function and most importantly affordability without compromise on quality is key to the foundation of my design philosophy.


Let me also assure you my positive personality will ensure that you achieve your ideal home in the most enjoyable and stress-free manner possible. So if you’re considering transforming your home today please feel free to give me a call or email for more info!

* Please NOTE: the price you pay is only for the planning and concept design service. It does not include the service for ordering furniture or any other items. Once the design scheme is purchased, implementation and completion of purchased scheme is your responsibility. R Interiors can assist you on the implementation of the design scheme but as an additional service.
